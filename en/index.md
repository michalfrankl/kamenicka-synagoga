Let’s work together to save the former synagogue and Evangelical prayer hall as a place of remembrance!

![](/assets/images/synagoga_okno_detail.jpg)

The synagogue in Kamenice nad Lipou, until recently used as a church by the Evangelical Church of Czech Brethren, is currently the only place in the town that commemorates Jewish history and the Holocaust. Our association aims to preserve this unique space and help transform it into a place of remembrance, education, and gathering that preserves the multicultural history of Kamenice and the wider region.

After World War II, the Evangelical Church of Czech Brethren helped preserve the former synagogue as a place of worship, thereby also keeping the memory of the Kamenice Jewish community destroyed during the Holocaust. Now, however, the building is threatened by a possible sale and conversion into a single-family home.
